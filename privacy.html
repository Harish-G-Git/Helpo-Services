Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Helpo Services collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Your name, phone number, and city when you submit a callback request
  • Business details when vendors register on the platform
  • Search terms and location for improving service suggestions
  • Your IP address, browser info, and usage data for analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Match you with relevant service providers
  • Help vendors respond to callback requests
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Communicate updates or support messages

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your name and phone number may be shared with vendors only when you explicitly request a callback.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to enhance browsing and analyze traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We use standard security practices to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

We may link to third-party sites or use tools (like Google Maps). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of callback services at any time

8. Children's Privacy

This platform is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at support@helposervices.com.
